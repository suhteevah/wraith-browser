## Metering

Usage is metered by **pages loaded** — each call to `browser_navigate` or equivalent MCP tool that loads a new page counts as one page. Navigating to an anchor on the same page, interacting with elements, or reading from the knowledge graph cache does not count.

Overages are billed at the per-page rate for your plan tier. You can set hard caps in your dashboard to prevent unexpected charges.

## FAQ

### Can I switch plans mid-cycle?

Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately and are prorated. Downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

### Is there an annual discount?

Annual billing is available on Starter, Growth, and Scale plans at a 20% discount. Contact sales for enterprise annual agreements.

### What happens if I exceed my page limit?

You can configure a hard cap or allow overages. Overage pages are billed at your plan's per-page rate. On the Free plan, requests are rate-limited once you hit the cap.

### Do self-hosted deployments count against the page limit?

Self-hosted deployments with a commercial license are metered the same way. You can run Wraith Browser on your own infrastructure and connect it to our metering API, or opt for an air-gapped Enterprise license with a fixed page allocation.
